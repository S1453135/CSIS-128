Movie Title O Brother, Where Art Thou?
Director/Producer Joel Coen
Leading Actor George Clooney
Leading Actress Holly Hunter
Rating PG-13
Brief Description In a 1930s-era retelling of Homer's "Odyssey", a trio of escaped convicts try to find buried treasure and ultimately get back "home," despite pursuit by a lawman. Along the way they encounter a man who will "give you money if you sing in his can," a lynch mob, sirens, horse-eating hillbillies, feuding governors on the campaign trail, a "geographical oddity," a bank robber, and more.
