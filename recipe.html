Beer Bread


A quick, easy, and delicious way to use up that cheap junky beer you had to have around for that last football party!

Ingredients

  • 3 c self-rising flour*
  • 0.5 c sugar
  • 12 oz beer, any kind
  • 0.25-0.5 stick butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350oF. Grease and flour a large loaf pan.
  2. Mix dry ingredients together in a large bowl.
  3. Add beer to dry ingredients and mix well into a sticky dough.
  4. Pour dough into loaf pan, and bake for 45 min.
  5. After 45 min, melt the butter and pour over the top of the bread. Return to oven for another 15 min.
  6. Remove from oven, allow to cool, then flip pan over to release bread. Try to let it completely cool before cutting...it's hard to resist but it makes cutting easier!
*If you don't have self-rising flour, use the following:
  • 3 c all-purporse flour
  • 4.5 tsp baking powder
  • 0.75 tsp salt
