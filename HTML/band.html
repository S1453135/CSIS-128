Puscifer

A slightly raunchy, odd collection of musicians led by Maynard James Keenan, who also leads Tool and A Perfect Circle.

Members

While the membership is described by Wikipedia as a revolving door, the group includes Maynard James Keenan (vocals), Carina Round (vocals, guitar, banjitar, and tambourine), Mat Mitchell (guitar), Paul Barker (bass), Jeff Friedl (drums and electronic samples), and Masha Zargaran (keyboards, samples, guitar, and backup vocals).

Sample of Discography

  • "V" Is for Vagina, the debut album of the group in 2007.
  • Conditions of My Parole, released in 2011, featuring collaborations of Keenan and 15 other artists.
  • Money Shot, released in 2015, featuring more vocal work by Carina Round than previously, and a noticeable desert theme. "Grand Canyon" is an excellent track.
