Welcome to the farm!

Storm Dancer Farm sits near Smithville Lake, just north of Kansas City MO. Follow us as we work to raise healthy, pastured poultry, beef cattle, and dairy goats, and improve the ecological function and beauty of the land.

Explore the farm history, meet our animals, see our trials and errors (and successes!) of on-farm projects, and connect with us. We are open to visitors - contact us to schedule a visit!
