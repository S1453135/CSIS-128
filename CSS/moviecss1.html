The Emperor's New Groove

Spoiled and foolish, Emperor Kuzco gets on the wrong side of his ambitious former advisor Yzma (secretly a sorceress of sorts) and is turned into a llama. To become human again, he must sneak back into his (now Yzma's) palace, but his only help is Pacha - a peasant whose village he had been planning to dismantle. What follows is a silly- funny adventure, true to Disney fashion, involving shoulder angels and devils, a diner in the middle of the jungle, and an evil kitten.

This movie ranks in my favorite films mostly because of the fun and memories associated with it. My college roommates and I used it as a post-prelim exam de-stressor. While the prelims were terrible, the movies were good, and we all just needed to laugh.

Main Cast of Characters and Voice Actors

  • Pacha: John Goodman
  • Yzma: Eartha Kitt
